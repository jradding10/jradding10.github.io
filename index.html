About Me

Nice to meet you! I'm Jacqueline Radding, a Master's student in Electrical Engineering at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo. I specialize in PCB design, digital communications, and more. Alongside my studies, I serve as the President of Cal Poly IEEE and work as a Resident Advisor, mentoring fellow students. My passion lies in developing IOT technologies and advancing research in V2V communication systems.
